Real-World Connection

Introduction: The Struggle with Course Management in Higher Education

University students juggle a multitude of responsibilities: attending lectures, completing assignments, preparing for exams, and managing extracurricular activities. Amidst this whirlwind, keeping track of past courses and fulfilling graduation requirements can become a significant burden. Traditional methods often involve cluttered spreadsheets, leading to confusion and missed opportunities.

The Problem: Disorganized Course Tracking and Missed Opportunities

In it's annual report, the Condition of Education 2009, the U.S. Department of Education described graduation rates for American students. A recent study by the National Center for Education Statistics found that 39% of undergraduate students take six or more years to graduate. One contributing factor is inefficient course planning. Students may forget prerequisites, miss registration deadlines for required courses, or struggle to identify electives that align with their interests and career goals.

Real-world examples illustrate these struggles. Sarah, a junior majoring in Computer Science, relies on a bulky binder to store her course syllabi. Finding specific prerequisites or remembering which professors she enjoyed becomes a time-consuming task. John, a sophomore, uses a cluttered spreadsheet to track his credits but struggles to visualize his progress towards graduation. Both Sarah and John would benefit from a streamlined system to organize their course history and plan for the future.

Our Course Tracker App: A Solution for Streamlined Course Management

Our Course Tracker application addresses these real-world problems by offering a centralized platform for students to manage their academic journeys. By providing a user-friendly interface for recording past courses, the app eliminates the need for paper records and cluttered spreadsheets. Students can easily access information like course names, semesters, professors, and grades.

Beyond Course History: Planning for a Successful Future

The optional feature of suggesting future courses takes the app a step further. By considering a student's interests, completed coursework, remaining semesters, and graduation requirements, the app can recommend relevant electives and required courses. This empowers students to plan their academic paths strategically, ensuring they graduate on time and with a well-rounded education.

Conclusion: Empowering Students for Academic Success

Our Course Tracker app directly addresses the real-world issue of disorganized course management faced by many university students. It provides them with a user-friendly tool to not only record past courses but also plan for a successful academic future. By streamlining course tracking and offering personalized recommendations, the app empowers students to make informed decisions, graduate on time, and maximize their academic potential.
